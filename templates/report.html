SALUTE Report Format

  • S - Size: Number of personnel or vehicles observed
  • A - Activity: What the enemy is currently doing
  • L - Location: Usually via grid coordinates
  • U - Unit/Uniform: Unit patches, insignia, or clothing description
  • T - Time: Time activity was observed (not report transmission)
  • E - Equipment: Weapons, vehicles, or special equipment observed
